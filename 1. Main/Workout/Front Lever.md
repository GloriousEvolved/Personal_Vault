## Requirements to next level: 

1. <span style="font-weight:700">Toes To Bar</span> - 10 consecutive(последовательных) reps with perfect form.
2.<span style="font-weight:700"> Tuck Front Lever</span> Hold - 15 seconds hold with perfect form and proper(надлежащий) engagement(взаимодействие?). *The tigther you are and the closer your knees are to chest - the easier. The more extended knees and the more you spread out - the harder.* 
2. <span style="font-weight:700">Upside Down Deadlift</span> - 10 consecutive reps with perfect form.
3. <span style="font-weight:700">Front Level Drops One Leg</span> - 6 reps on each leg consecutively.
4. <span style="font-weight:700">Front Level Drops Both Legs</span> - 10 consecutive reps with perfect form.
5. <span style="font-weight:700">Front Lever Raises One Leg</span> - 6 reps on each leg.
6. <span style="font-weight:700">Front Lever Raises Both Legs</span> - 10 reps.
